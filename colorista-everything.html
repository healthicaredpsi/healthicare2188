Colorista by HealthiCare

Colorista is an event, where our users forget about all their everyday problems and showcase themselves in any form of art. Colorista is an annual event with exiciting prizes which are disclosed on the day of results itself. Colorista is just a way for people to get busy with something which is "not important" unlike all other things in life!
